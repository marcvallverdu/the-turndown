# The Turndown SEO/content foundation

## Audience
Design-aware luxury travelers comparing hotels by atmosphere, service, architecture, destination fit, and whether the stay justifies its friction and cost.

## Voice
Editorial, specific, slightly dry, and decisive. Prefer concise judgment over generic luxury adjectives. The page should help a reader choose.

## Do not claim
- Firsthand stays, meals, rates, availability, awards, renovations, openings, or service incidents unless already supported by site data or a named source.
- Live prices or availability.
- Paid inclusion, anonymous inspection mechanics, or certainty the site cannot prove.

## Differentiation
The Turndown is useful when it explains the tradeoff: who should book, who should skip, what the hotel is emotionally good at, and where the friction is.
